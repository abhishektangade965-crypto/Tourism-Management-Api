Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 16, 2026

1. Data Collection Policy

We collect personal information necessary to offer and manage travel bookings. This includes names, email addresses, phone numbers, passport details (for international bookings), and payment confirmation. We do not sell or lease your personal data.

2. Security Architectures & Hashing

Your passwords are encrypted using **BCrypt cryptographic hashing** before they are stored in our databases. BCrypt incorporates a random salt and adaptive workload factors, rendering them highly resistant to brute-force and pre-computation attacks.

3. Authentication & JWT Tokens

Our application utilizes **JSON Web Tokens (JWT)** to authorize user sessions securely. When you log in, the API yields a stateless signed token containing your credentials. This token is stored in the browser's local storage and transmitted via the Authorization: Bearer header.

Note on JWT Security: Please note that JWT tokens function solely as a session transport mechanism for stateless API authorization. They do not encrypt user data stored on our servers. Server-side security, firewall configurations, parameter checking, and database encryption are implemented independently to protect user records from unauthorized access.

4. HTTPS Encryption & Transmission

All network transmissions between your browser and our Spring Boot servers are strictly encrypted over **HTTPS using TLS (Transport Layer Security)**. This prevents eavesdropping, tampering, or man-in-the-middle attacks.

5. Secure Storage Practices

User profiles, bookings, and package details are stored within a secured database structure protected by network access control lists (ACLs) and restricted backend services. We do not save credit card details locally; all payment transactions are handled through standard tokenized financial integrations.

6. User Rights

Under applicable data protection frameworks, you maintain rights to access your records, request data correction or deletion, withdraw consent for promotional operations, and receive copy files of your account details. To exercise these rights, contact our privacy officer at privacy@tourismhub.com.
