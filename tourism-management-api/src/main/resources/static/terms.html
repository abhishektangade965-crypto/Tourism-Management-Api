Effective Date: June 16, 2026

1. Booking Policies

All bookings completed via Tourism Hub are subject to package availability and verification. You must submit valid passport details, name records, and contact information. A booking is considered confirmed only upon receipt of full payment and the issuance of a valid booking confirmation code.

2. Cancellation Rules

Travelers can request cancellations directly through their Customer Dashboard. Cancellations trigger automatically calculated refund eligibility based on the time remaining before the scheduled check-in:

  • 14 days or more before check-in: Full cancellation permitted.
  • 7 to 13 days before check-in: Partial cancellation permitted.
  • Under 7 days before check-in: Cancellation requests will not clear refunds.

3. Refund Policies

Eligible refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5 to 10 business days. We do not store your credit card credentials; all financial settlements are processed via secure tokenized banking systems.

4. User Responsibilities

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials (password and username) and restricting unauthorized local access. You agree to ensure that all passport numbers, contact telephone values, and visa details submitted to the service are current, complete, and legally correct.

5. Liability Clauses

Tourism Hub acts as an booking platform and concierge provider. We maintain no liability for flight cancellations, luggage losses, weather delays, or personal injury occurring during physical travel. All disputes are governed by the local state laws where our headquarters reside.
