Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my tour package booking? expand_more

You can cancel your bookings directly through your Customer Dashboard. Log in, find your booking in the "Upcoming Trips" or history, and click "Cancel". Alternatively, contact our 24/7 concierge lines. Diffs apply depending on your time to departure.

What is the refund policy? expand_more

Cancellations made 14 days or more before the check-in date receive a 100% refund. Cancellations made between 7 and 13 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made under 7 days are non-refundable.

Are flights included in the price? expand_more

Flight inclusions depend on the specific package. Luxury tier packages (like Maldives Serenity Escape) include domestic flights, whereas adventure treks do not. Please read the package details in the "Tour Packages" catalog before booking.
